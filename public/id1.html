Flower Snow...
With my classical piano background,
I have been writing and singing
bilingual folk and pop music in English and Korean since I was 18 years old.

My music is influenced by my experiences; growing up with family and friends in the countryside in the small town, Samga, Korea  
and moving into a big America city...
 
The song you are listening to, "Flower Snow," was written for my grandmother.
The hardworking, dusty farms, of 
my small hometown, Samga are surrounded by beautiful Cherryblossom flowers, and fed by the Yangcheon river.
 
When my grandmother passed away in April,
the cherryblossom flower petals started
scattering everywhere in Samga like
white snow in Spring.

I was very sad that I lost my grandmother;
however, it was one of the most beautiful moments that I have ever seen. I am so happy that all people can listen to this lovely story of my hometown and picture the flower petals falling.
